Web XR - GeoDomes

I’m launching a series of interactive called GeoDomes. You can put these anywhere in the world as long as you know the co-ordinates but you can also use am in image or marker to trigger the GeoDome to appear. I built an interface to make it easy for anyone to create a geodome. Just 3 buttons and your done
